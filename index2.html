Reviews of My Favourite Movies/Books/TV Shows/Music Concerts

  1. Movie: 3 Idiots

    3 Idiots is an iconic Indian film that blends comedy with a powerful message about the educational system. It focuses on following one's passion and challenging societal expectations, with memorable performances by Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, and Sharman Joshi.

  2. Book: The White Tiger by Aravind Adiga

    The White Tiger is a gripping novel that tells the story of Balram Halwai, a poor Indian man who rises from a village servant to a successful entrepreneur. Through dark humor and sharp critique, the book explores the themes of social class and corruption in modern India.

  3. Music Concert: AR Rahman Live Concert

    AR Rahman's live concert is a mesmerizing experience for any music lover. His mastery in blending Indian classical music with contemporary sounds creates an unforgettable atmosphere. His performances of iconic songs, such as those from "Dil Se" and "Roja," leave the audience spellbound.
